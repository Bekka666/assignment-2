Welcome!

Come visit the Orleans Indoor Pool - with lots of activities for the whole family. Enjoy the gripping water slide or swing off the tarzan rope into a recreational 30 meter pool. Parents, enjoy quality time with your children and the ability to socialize with other parents or relax and take a break in the Sauna. The Orleans Indoor Pool is completely wheelchair accessible, and affordable to all.

The Orleans Indoor Pool offers many aquatic courses and activities for everyone to enjoy. Swimming lessons, water aerobics, lap time or even just a simple outing with the family – you name it, we provide it!
